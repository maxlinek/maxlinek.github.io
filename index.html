About Me

I work as Staff Data Analyst at Tier Mobility on improving and scaling experimentation, as well as on high-priority impact evaluations with observational data.

In 2021, I completed my Ph.D. in Economics at the Hertie School and the Berlin School of Economics. My dissertation is titled Measures to Influence Individual Behavior: Four Essays in Economics.

Previous to my doctoral studies, I worked for an international management consultancy.
